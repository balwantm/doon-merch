About Us

Welcome to DOON – where innovation meets style! Born from the minds of a group of ambitious students from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Patna (IIT PATNA). United by a passion for innovation and a love for quality merchandise, we embarked on a journey to redefine the landscape of fashion and lifestyle accessories.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to create products that seamlessly blend functionality with style. With a commitment to excellence and a relentless pursuit of perfection, we set out to design and curate a collection that reflects our vision for the modern lifestyle.

FDriven by a spirit of entrepreneurship and fueled by the energy of youth, we strive to push the boundaries of creativity and set new standards in the world of merchandise. Our team of talented designers and artisans work tirelessly to bring you products that not only elevate your style quotient but also enhance your everyday experiences.

As a company founded by students, we understand the importance of staying true to our roots while embracing the challenges of the future. We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation and collaboration, where ideas flourish and dreams take flight.

Join us on our journey as we continue to explore new horizons, push the limits of possibility, and inspire others to do the same. Lets make DOON a testament to the spirit of creativity and excellence.
